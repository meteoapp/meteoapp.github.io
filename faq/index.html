Frequently asked questions (FAQs)

Does Météo work on iPhone and iPad?

Yes! Météo was built and designed to work on all devices running iOS 9 and above.

Why is the app free?

I wanted to bring the best app to the most people possible without making them purchase the app and without hiding the best features behind in-app purchases.

Which weather locations are available?

Météo utilizes data from Environment and Climate Change Canada. You can see a full list of weather locations on their weather site.
